Each playoff game is two sessions with a one-hour lunch break. Overall winners include
gold points! Awards are 100% gold for Flights A and B, and 25% gold for Flight C.

If you qualified in multiple flights, you may choose to play in any flight for which
you are eligible. If you are eligible to play in Flight C, but qualified at the club
level in Flight B, you may play in either B or C flight during the playoffs.

For the Unit and District playoffs, you may play with any partner who has also qualified
at the club level &mdash; but you must play with the same partner at the Unit, District,
and National levels.
