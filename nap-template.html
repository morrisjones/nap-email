Congratulations! You have qualified to advance in District 23's North American Pairs competition. This is your invitation to compete in one of two Unit Final games, where you may qualify for the District Final in January. Winners at the District Final will represent District 23 at the North American Bridge Championships next spring in Philadelphia!

You are eligible to play in one of two Unit Final games. If you qualified for more than one flight, you could play in both Unit Finals, in a different flight for each event. 50% of the field will advance to the District Final in January.

Unit Final 1:
Sunday, October 15, 10:00 a.m.
South Bay Bridge Club, Lomita, CA
Unit Final 2:
Sunday, November 5, 10:00 a.m.
Long Beach Bridge Club, Long Beach, CA

Each playoff game is two sessions with a one-hour lunch break. Overall winners include gold points! Awards are 100% gold for Flights A and B, and 25% gold for Flight C.

If you qualified in multiple flights, you may choose to play in any flight for which you are eligible. If you are eligible to play in Flight C, but qualified at the club level in Flight B, you may play in either B or C flight during the playoffs.

For the Unit and District finals, you may play with any partner who has also qualified at the previous level — but you must play with the same partner in Philadelphia with whom you played at the District Final.

Please pre-register online to play in a Unit Final

Because of the large number of qualifiers, we need to know if you're coming! Visit the District 23 North American Pairs site at https://nap.bridgemojo.com

On the site, you can check results from all club-level qualifying games.

Either member of a partnership may register for the partnership, and provide an email contact. Alternatively, please send an email to nap@bridgemojo.com with this information:

  • Unit Final game you wish to play in
  • Name of both members of the partnership
  • Flight entering (A, B, or C)
